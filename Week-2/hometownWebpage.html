Hometown Webpage

Nagpur, India

Location

Nagpur is located 478 miles east of Mumbai.

About

Weather

Nagpur is the winter capital of the state of Maharashtra.

Population

Around 3,047,000 people call Nagpur home.

Sports

Cricket and football are popular sports in Nagpur.

Things to do

  • Witness the sunset at Futala Lake
  • Go on an Orange orchard tour
  • Learn about space at the Raman Science Centre
  • Stand At ‘The Center Of India’
